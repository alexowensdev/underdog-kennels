FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Cancellation Policy

We are a hotel (a dog hotel) and we have a Cancellation Policy just like a people hotel We require notification of an overnight dog boarding reservation cancellation at least 24 hours prior. An amount equal to one nights stay will be charged if notification is not received within this time frame.

Holiday Cancellation Policy

For all National Holidays and Spring Breaks, an amount equal to 2 nights stay will be charged if notification is not received at least 72 hours prior to the start of your reservation. We require a credit card to be on file with us at the time a reservation is made to uphold this policy and your card will be automatically charged.

Vaccination & Health Requirements

All guests must be a MINIMUM of 4 MONTHS OLD and FULLY VACCINATED with Rabies, Distemper, Parvo and 6 Month Bordetella (a 12 Month Bordetella is accepted if it’s been received in the last 6 months otherwise it would need to be updated before your dog can enter our facility).

All guests over 6 months of age must be spayed or neutered (altered) to play in Doggy Daycare (pregnancy and aggression due to hormones are issues which we guard against).

No unneutered (unaltered) Male Dogs over the age of 2 are permitted.
